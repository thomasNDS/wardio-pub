Wardio

A desktop League of Legends companion — meta runes, builds and tier lists in champion select, a live in-game overlay with objective timers, and your own profile synced from the client.

Pre-game

Runes, summoner spells, item path, skill order, tier and counters for your pick — with one-click rune import.

Champions

Blitz-style build pages and a sortable tier list (win rate, pick, ban, matches) across every role.

In-game

A transparent overlay with Dragon / Baron / Herald timers, plus a live scoreboard and matchup counters.

Profile

Your Riot ID, level and ranked, auto-synced from the running client — no password, no key.

Public data

Recommendation dataset

Wardio's runes / builds / tiers / counters are served as a single static JSON, refreshed weekly. The app fetches it directly; you can too.

https://thomasnds.github.io/wardio-pub/curated.json
